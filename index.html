About leve.

Leve Copenhagen is a Copenhagen-based clothing company that has a clear mission to deliver unique quality products at manageable prices.

Therefore, our clothes are not mass-produced, but come in small collections with a focus on quality and price, so you get a unique product that only quite a few are in possession of.

With our limited collections, we also avoid unnecessary residual stocks and thus an unnecessary burden on the environment.
